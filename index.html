INTERNET SECURITY GLOSSARY

malware - malicious software

virus - a program that you need to let in that replicates itself and infects code with its own

worm - a virus that worms its way into your computer without asking you

botnets - your computer gets infected and taken over and it then becomes a part of a big network of other bots

spyware - software that spies on you and your computer

hacking - getting unauthorized access to a computer system

DDoS - distributed denial of service - flooding servers with a lot of traffic

packet filter firewalls - simple firewalls that check packets

proxy server firewall - a server firewall that acts as a gate and maintains your privacy with a different IP address to your own

anti-virus software - software that detects, prevents, and removes malware and other stuff
